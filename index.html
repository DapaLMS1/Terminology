Prefix

Appears at the beginning, describes location, time, number, or intensity.

Hypo-

Meaning: Under (or below normal)

Example Term: Hypothermia

Definition: below normal body temperature

Pre-

Meaning: Before

Example Term: Preoperative

Definition: before surgery

Peri-

Meaning: Around, surrounding

Example Term: Pericoronitis

Definition: Inflammation around a tooth crown

Sub-

Meaning: Under, below

Example Term: Subgingival

Definition: Beneath the gumline

Intra-

Meaning: Within, inside

Example Term: Intraoral

Definition: Within the mouth

Hyper-

Meaning: Excessive, above normal

Example Term: Hyperplasia

Definition: Overgrowth of tissue

Hypo-

Meaning: Below normal, deficient

Dental Example: Hypocalcification

Definition: Insufficient calcium in enamel

Dys-

Meaning: Difficult, abnormal

Example Term: Dysphagia

Definition: Difficulty swallowing

Para-

Meaning: Beside, near

Example Term: Parotid

Definition: Near the ear (salivary gland region)

Root Word

The base of the term, usually referring to a body part, structure, or system.

Dent-/Odont-

Meaning: Tooth

Example Term: Dentition, Orthodontics

Definition: Arrangement of teeth; tooth alignment

Gingiv-

Meaning: Gums

Example Term: Gingivitis

Definition: Inflammation of gums

Enamel / Amel-

Meaning: Enamel

Example Term: Hypo-amelogenesis

Definition: Under-developed enamel

Pulp-

Meaning: Dental pulp

Example Term: Pulpitis

Definition: Inflammation of dental pulp

Alveol-

Meaning: Socket / bone cavity

Example Term: Alveolar ridge

Definition: Bone supporting teeth

Or- / Stomat-

Meaning: Mouth

Example Term: Oral hygiene, Stomatitis

Definition: Mouth care; inflammation of the mouth

Maxill- / Mandibul-

Meaning: Jaw

Example Term: Maxillary arch, Mandibular nerve

Definition: Upper / lower jaw structures

Cephal- / Cran-

Meaning: Head / skull

Example Term: Cephalometric analysis

Definition: Head measurement for orthodontics

Cardi-

Meaning: Heart

Related Terms: Cardiology, Cardiogram

Definition: Study of the heart; heart measurement record

Suffix

Comes at the end, usually describes a condition, disease, or procedure.

-itis

Meaning: Inflammation

Example: Gingivitis

Definition: Inflammation of the gums

-ectomy

Meaning: Surgical removal

Example: Odontectomy

Definition: Removal of a tooth

-algia / -dynia

Meaning: Pain

Example: Odontalgia

Definition: Toothache

-osis

Meaning: Condition, usually abnormal

Example: Halitosis

Definition: Bad breath condition

-plasty

Meaning: Surgical repair

Example: Gingivoplasty

Definition: Reshaping of gum tissue

-logy / -logist

Meaning: Study / specialist

Example: Periodontologist

Definition: Specialist in gum diseases

-gram / -graph

Meaning: Record / instrument

Example: Radiograph

Definition: Image produced by X-rays
