Building Blocks of Dental Terminology:

  • Prefix: Appears at the beginning of a word and usually indicates location, time, amount, or number. Example: *sub-* (under).
  • Root Words: The foundation of the word, providing its essential meaning, often referring to a body part, organ, or substance. Example: *gingiv-* (gums).
  • Suffix: Appears at the end of a word and often indicates a procedure, condition, disease, or study. Example: *-itis* (inflammation).
Peri-

Meaning:

Around, surrounding

For example, Pericoronitis (Inflammation around a tooth crown).
Sub-

Meaning:

Under, below

For example, Subgingival (Beneath the gumline).
Intra-

Meaning:

Within, inside

For example, Intraoral (Within the mouth).
Hyper-

Meaning:

Excessive, above normal

For example, Hyperplasia (Overgrowth of tissue).
Hypo-

Meaning:

Below normal, deficient

For example, Hypocalcification (Insufficient calcium in enamel).
Dys-

Meaning:

Difficult, abnormal

For example, Dysphagia (Difficulty swallowing).
Para-

Meaning:

Beside, near

For example, Parotid (Near the ear - salivary gland region).
Brady-

Meaning:

Slow

For example, Bradycardia (Slow heart rate).
Tachy-

Meaning:

Fast

For example, Tachypnoea (Rapid breathing).
Hemi-

Meaning:

Half

For example, Hemiplegia (Paralysis of one side).
Poly-

Meaning:

Many / much

For example, Polyuria (Excessive urination).
gingiv-

Meaning:

Gums

For example, Gingivitis is inflammation of the gums.
odont-

Meaning:

Tooth

For example, Odontology is the study of teeth.
amel-

Meaning:

Enamel

For example, Amelogenesis is the formation of enamel.
cardi-

Meaning:

Heart

For example, Cardiology is the study of the heart.
hem- / hemat-

Meaning:

Blood

For example, Hemostasis is the stoppage of blood flow.
Derm / Cutane-

Meaning:

Skin

For example, Dermatitis (Skin inflammation).
Hepat-

Meaning:

Liver

For example, Hepatitis (Inflammation of the liver).
Osteo-

Meaning:

Bone

For example, Osteoporosis (Porous bone condition).
My / Myo-

Meaning:

Muscle

For example, Myocardium (Heart muscle).
-Algia / -dynia

Meaning:

Pain

For example, Odontalgia (Toothache).
-Ectomy

Meaning:

Surgical removal

For example, Appendectomy (Removal of the appendix).
-Gram / -graph

Meaning:

Record / Instrument for Recording

For example, Radiograph (Image produced by X-rays).
-Itis

Meaning:

Inflammation

For example, Gingivitis (Inflammation of the gums).
-Logy / -logist

Meaning:

Study / Specialist in

For example, Periodontologist (Specialist in gum diseases).
-Oma

Meaning:

Tumour / growth

For example, Carcinoma (A cancerous growth).
-Osis

Meaning:

Condition, usually abnormal

For example, Halitosis (Bad breath condition).
-Pathy

Meaning:

Disease

For example, Neuropathy (Nerve disease).
-Plasty

Meaning:

Surgical repair, reshaping

For example, Gingivoplasty (Reshaping of gum tissue).
